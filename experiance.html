Hi, I'm Mark Zuckerberg

I co-founded Facebook in 2004 from my dorm room at Harvard University, where I was studying computer science. My goal was to create a platform that connects people worldwide. Over the years, Facebook has grown into one of the largest social media networks globally, with billions of users. I'm passionate about technology and innovation, and I continue to lead Facebook as its CEO, focusing on advancing social media, virtual reality, and internet connectivity.

Awards: Axel Springer Award

Books: Mark Zuckerberg, Open Form: Facebook Artists in Residence, 2012-2017

Organizations founded: Meta, Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, Meta AI, Startup:Education, FWD.us

Outside of work, I'm dedicated to philanthropy through the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, which I founded with my wife, Priscilla Chan.
